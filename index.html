Research

I work at the interface of general relativity and quantum theory, with a focus on covariant phase space methods, boundary symmetries and charges, and models of quantum/semiclassical black holes. My research interests include covariant phase space and surface charges in gravity, quantum black holes (information flow, evaporation, near-horizon symmetries), spin networks and spinfoams, discrete geometry and renormalization, and geometric quantisation. Selected collaborators: CASPER group EHU, Computational Mathematics DeustoTech.
