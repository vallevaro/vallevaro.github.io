Research

I work at the interface of general relativity and quantum theory, with a focus on covariant phase space methods, boundary symmetries and charges, and models of quantum/semiclassical black holes. I am also interested in quantum control and learning for physics-inspired systems.

  • Covariant phase space and surface charges in gravity.
  • Quantum black holes: information flow, evaporation, near-horizon symmetries.
  • Spin networks / spinfoams; discrete geometry and renormalization.
  • Control theory and machine learning for quantum dynamics.
